1. Start-of-Turn Effects
   - Weather and terrain effects activate (e.g. Sandstorm, Electric Terrain).
   - Room effects like Trick Room or Gravity are checked.
   - Field hazards (Spikes, Stealth Rock, Sticky Web) trigger on switch-ins.
 
2. Forced Switches
   - If a Pokémon fainted last turn, its trainer must select a replacement.
   - Abilities like Natural Cure or Regenerator may activate on switch-out.
 
3. Ability Activation (Passive)
   - On switch-in: Intimidate, Download, Drizzle, etc.
   - Some abilities trigger based on field state (e.g. Forecast, Mimicry).
 
4. Item Activation (Start-of-Turn)
   - Air Balloon pops if hit last turn.
   - Room Service activates if Trick Room is active.
   - Terrain Seeds activate if terrain is up.     
 
5. Move Selection
   - Players choose moves, switches, or items (in single-player).
   - Speed is calculated (including modifiers like Paralysis, Tailwind, Trick Room).
 
6. Move Execution (Fastest to Slowest)
   - Pre-move abilities/items: Prankster, Quick Claw, Custap Berry.
   - Move resolution:
     - Accuracy check.
     - Damage calculation.
     - Secondary effects (status, stat drops, flinches).
     - Multi-hit or multi-turn move handling.
   - Contact-based effects: Static, Iron Barbs, Rocky Helmet.
   - Item consumption: Berries (e.g. Sitrus, Lum), Focus Sash, Weakness Policy.
   - Ability triggers: Emergency Exit, Stamina, Gooey, etc.
 
7. Aftermath Effects
   - Aftermath, Rough Skin, Cursed Body, etc.
   - Red Card, Eject Button, Eject Pack may force switches.
 
8. End-of-Turn Effects
   - Status damage: Burn, Poison, Badly Poisoned.
   - Weather damage: Hail, Sandstorm.
   - Healing: Leftovers, Black Sludge, Grassy Terrain, Aqua Ring.
   - Leech Seed, Ingrain, Wish resolution.
   - Perish Song countdown.
   - End-of-turn abilities: Speed Boost, Moody, Harvest.
   - Held item regeneration: Pickup, Recycle.
 
9. Fainting Check
   - Pokémon that hit 0 HP faint.
   - If both sides lose their last Pokémon, tie rules apply.
 
10. Victory Check
    - If one side has no remaining Pokémon, the battle ends.
